Privacy Policy

2. Information We Collect

In compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, we lawfully process the following information:

  • QR Code Scan History: Stored only on device local storage
  • App Preferences: Dark mode, language settings, etc. (local storage only)
  • Technical Data: Anonymized crash reports (for improvement purposes only)

Important: Camera access is used only for QR code scanning; no images or videos are stored.

3. Legal Basis and Purpose

Personal data processing is based on:

  • Contract Performance: Providing app services
  • Legitimate Interest: App stability improvement, technical issue resolution
  • Consent: Optional features (configurable in settings)

4. Data Retention and Location

All data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Scan History: Until user deletion or app uninstallation
  • Settings: Automatically deleted upon app uninstallation
  • No data transmission to external servers

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Right to Erasure: Use in-app history deletion feature
  • Data Portability: QR history export functionality
  • Right to Object: Disable history saving in settings
  • Transparency: Clear explanation through this policy

6. Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • On-device encryption for data storage
  • No network communication to prevent data breaches
  • Regular security audits
  • Compliance with latest iOS security standards

7. Third-Party Sharing

We never share your data with third parties, except:

  • Legal obligations (court orders, etc.)
  • Your explicit consent

8. International Data Transfers

No international data transfers occur as the app processes data locally only.

9. Children's Privacy

If you are under 13, please obtain parental consent before use. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Policy Changes

Material changes to this policy will be notified via in-app notification or email. Changes take effect 30 days after notification.
